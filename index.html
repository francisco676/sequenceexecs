Where the leverage is

Sequence is defining financial routing. Gilad's voice is the moat that compounds.

You said it on the call: distribution is everything, and trust is the layer that doesn't commoditize. Sequence isn't a feature in someone else's category, it's a category you're naming. "Financial router" is your line. The risk isn't that the product gets out-built. The risk is that the category gets named by someone else while Gilad waits to start posting.

Why Gilad's voice is the lever, structurally.

  • It's the only differentiator that compounds. A $7.5M round announces once. A 600% growth stat lands once. Gilad posting in his voice, naming financial routing for 9 months, builds a body of work prospects, partners, and VCs can search, validate, and quote back at their internal champion.

Gilad as the named voice is the opposite. The more places he shows up, the sharper the wedge, because every post traces back to one operator with one thesis.
